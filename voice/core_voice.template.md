# Core Voice Template

Use this file to capture the prose traits you want to preserve across writing sessions.

## Traits To Keep

- preferred sentence length and rhythm
- tolerance for compression versus explanation
- preferred level of directness
- phrases or constructions you avoid
- how strongly you state causal claims when the design supports them

## Non-Negotiables

- never fabricate evidence
- keep claims proportional to design strength
- prefer concrete nouns and verbs over filler abstractions
